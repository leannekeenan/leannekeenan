<h1>Beauté Salon</h1>

<p>
Beauté Salon's site was created to provide a professional web presence to the salon and assist in managing appointments, 
providing customers the option to view availbe services, prices, staff specialties, and promotional items improving customer 
service.  The site has also provided the opportunity for clients to have the choice to call in or book online for their 
appointment.
</p>

<span>
Link to project can be found here: <a href="https://beautesalon.netlify.app">https://beautesalon.netlify.app</a>

<h2>
How It's Made:
</h2>

<p>
HTML5 practices were used to buid this site ensuring that the correct semantic tag was used to define the indivivual sections
of the page. The CSS was designed mostly around the use of flexboxes and grids to ensure the smoothest transitions when 
switching between viewports.  The site is intended to illustrate the salons high quality staff, the versitile services they
provide and a more up-to-date style of booking appointments to help bring the salos booking process into the present.  The site
is also meant to help the salon support products and brands they have as retail opportunities within the store or offer 
promotions for services and specials.  The navigation on the site was build using JavaScript as well as the markup languages 
to provide a collapsing nav bar providing a clean main page that does not distract from the salons presented mission statement
and promotions.
</p>

<h2>
Lessons Learned:
</h2>

<p>
This site provided an opportunity to work with JavaScript in a more realistic way and apply it to an everyday object
found on almost every website running today.  The project also gave me a cahance to work more with grids and find new ways to
present content on a page outside of margin alterations.
</p>
