Welcome to Sevynashyuns: A World of Sin and Strategy

In the land of Sevynashyuns, seven city-states vie for power, each driven by one of the seven deadly sins. From the militaristic might of Iratia (Wrath) to the shadowy dealings of Avarice (Greed), this world is filled with complex characters, fragile alliances, and fierce rivalries.

This website serves as a comprehensive resource for all the characters, factions, and histories that shape Sevynashyuns. Whether you're a player, a Dungeon Master, or just curious about this intricate world, you'll find everything you need to immerse yourself in the ongoing story.

Explore the Characters

Meet the key figures of Sevynashyuns: from war generals to criminal masterminds, entertainers to scholars. Each character has a rich backstory, complex motivations, and deep connections to the world around them.

Discover the Factions

Learn about the seven city-states that define the politics and power struggles of Sevynashyuns. Each city represents one of the deadly sins, and their interactions will shape the fate of the world.

Trace the History

Delve into the timeline of major events that have led to the current state of Sevynashyuns. From ancient wars to recent betrayals, the history of this land is written by both blood and ambition.

Character Relationships

Unravel the intricate web of alliances, rivalries, and betrayals that bind these characters together. Discover who holds power, who seeks vengeance, and who walks the thin line between friend and foe.

Landmarks and Places

Sevynashyuns is a world rich in history and intrigue, with legendary landmarks and places of power that hold deep significance to the city-states. From the towering walls of The Bastion of Iratia to the opulent halls of The Golden Bazaar in Avarice, each location tells a story of its own.

Explore mystical ruins, ancient fortresses, and bustling markets as you journey through the lands. These places not only serve as important locations for quests and battles but also reveal the deeper secrets of Sevynashyuns’ past and future.

Homebrew Items

The world of Sevynashyuns is filled with powerful and unique magical items, crafted by legendary artisans, infused with ancient magic, or forged in the heat of war. Explore a collection of homebrew items designed specifically for this campaign—artifacts that can turn the tide of battle or unlock hidden potential.

From the elusive Blade of Avarice—a cursed sword that grants immense power at the cost of greed—to the mysterious Cradle of Lust, a relic that tempts those who wield it with visions of their deepest desires, each item holds the potential for both glory and downfall.

Players can uncover these rare items through quests, battles, and interactions with key NPCs, making each artifact a pivotal part of the Sevynashyuns story.
