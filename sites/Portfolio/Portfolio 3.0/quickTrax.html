QuickTrax Time Tracking App (Wireframe)

With QuickTrax, tracking clock-in and clock-out times, managing breaks, and processing time off requests has never been easier. Our user-friendly and intuitive app simplifies these essential tasks, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – your business.

About the Wireframe

I was inspired to create this app from my time working at Allied Universal @ META. Since we still used paper time sheets to clock officers time and time off requests, lots of errors tended to occur and paperwork often went mising. I wanted to create a program to solve that issue and QuickTrax is my attempt at a solution.

The wireframe is illustrated from top to bottom showing the main page at the top and the time off request form as the last page, as it will be what populates when the'TOR' tab is clicked (as it is the last nav tab, it was organized as the last page).

The main page has a company logo in the upper left corner and a log in button in the upper right. Visitors must log in before other pages can be accesses. When the login buttin is clicked the login in lage will pooulate asking the user to enter their username and password. For simplicity, a registere option has been included in the login in window which allows user to acces the register page which asks users to create a username, enter a email for the account,a s well as enter and verify a password. Once registered or logged in, users are direceted to the clock in / clock out page.

The clockin / clock out page will allow users to enter their name, ID, date, the campus they are logging into and a button to timestamp their clock in. the data is them printed to a tablwe within the app, as well it is sent to a universal excel file that is sent to the management team. That excel file can then be shared across all campuses to help leadership know what officer is where, and.know if and when officers have taken their breaks.

The breaks tab will allow oficers similar function as the clockin / clock out page, meaning they will be able to select a break tab and have the time in and out be printed to the app. This will provide officers a timestamp of when they are meant to return from break. A countdown clock will also be provided to illustrate to the user how much time they have left in their breaks. This will help in reducing tardiness and providing more personal responsibility to your team.Similarily to the clockin / clock out page, the breaks will also be sent to a excel time sheet shared across campuses to help keep track of what officer is where, find resourse officers that can help complete breaks on time, and ensure officers are taking breaks at the proper time.

Lastly time time off request tab will provide a TOR for for officers to complete and sign before sending to theor leadership team. The file will be saved as a PDF and will be emailed back to the officer will approval or denial. This will reduce the number of lost time off requests, ensure the priority of the request goes to the first to make teh request, and help officers keep an eye on their time off requests throughout the year.
