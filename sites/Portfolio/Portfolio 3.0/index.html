Who I Am

I grew up in the vibrant Bay Area, where I had the privilege of witnessing the remarkable rise of Silicon Valley. Surrounded by groundbreaking innovations and technological advancements, I developed a deep fascination for the digital realm. It was this fascination that led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, with a strong determination to embark on a career as a front-end developer.

After graduating from college, I set out to pursue my passion and took on freelance projects while simultaneously working full-time in security at META. This unique combination allowed me to gain invaluable real-world experience while still exploring my creative side as a developer. It was during this time that I stumbled upon numerous opportunities to design and build projects that aimed to improve our everyday lives. I saw the potential to create practical solutions that could make a genuine impact, and I dedicated my spare time to bringing these ideas to life.

Driven by my entrepreneurial spirit, I eventually made the decision to transition back to full-time freelance work. This step allowed me to fully immerse myself in the world of web development, taking on diverse projects and honing my skills further. However, as my journey progressed, I realized that I yearned for a greater global influence and the chance to collaborate with like-minded professionals.

Today, I find myself eagerly seeking a new chapter in my career, where I can join a company that offers a stimulating environment and a clear career path with a global reach. I am passionate about contributing my expertise in front-end development to a team that shares my vision for pushing boundaries and making a meaningful impact on a larger scale.

I am excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead and the opportunity to create exceptional digital experiences that resonate with users worldwide. Let's connect and explore how we can collaborate to bring innovative ideas to life and shape the future of technology together.
