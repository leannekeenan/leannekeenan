Time Management Hub

This digital platform is designed to streamline the process of clocking in and out, logging breaks and lunches, and managing your work locations throughout your shift. Our goal is to ensure accurate time tracking and efficient workflow management for all employees. Please review the following guidelines carefully before using the system:

  1. Clocking In and Out: Use the system to clock in at the start of your shift and clock out at the end. Ensure that you are clocked into the correct campus.
  2. Logging Breaks and Lunches Log your breaks and lunch periods accurately. The system allows you to record the start and end times of each break.
  3. Changing Campus Locations: If you need to change the campus location during your shift, use the system to update your clock-in location. This ensures accurate tracking of your work location.
  4. Real-Time Updates: All time entries and changes are recorded in real-time and sent to the data analytics page for review and analysis.
  5. Accuracy and Compliance: Ensure that all time entries are accurate and comply with company policies. Any discrepancies should be reported to your manager immediately.

Your cooperation in accurately recording your work hours helps us maintain a well-organized and efficient workplace.
