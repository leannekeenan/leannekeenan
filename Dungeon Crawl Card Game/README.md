<h1>Dungeon Crawl: A Card Game</h1>

<p>
  Inspired by the TTRPG gaming mechanic created by Micha Risenger of "Tales from the Stinky Dragon"

   Embark on an adventure as you navigate through your DM's treacherous dungeon represented by this deck of cards. 
   Players must roll a 20-sided die to meat or beat the DC of the dungeon and strategically choose from five action 
   choices to progress safely. Then players choose a card from the river to determine what happens. Will you and 
   your party find the right way out? Encounter monsters? Find immeasureable treasure? The options are endless with this
   card game where you will never play the same dungeon twice! 
</p>
