How to Play

Instructions

  1. Roll for iniative to determine who will chose a card in the river first.

  2. Find a number of "Right Path" cards as decided by the DM.

    • Provides added difficulty to your dungeon [its recommended you start with finding three and add from there]

  3. Choose cards in the river to progress through the dungeon. Beware of traps and monsters!

    • See Card Types for Card Effects

  4. The DM will determine what happens to the party in the dungeon based on the card drawn in the river[check the card types for details]

  5. All three cards in the river must be played before the DM draws a new river

  6. Once all cards in the river are shown, and playthrough is complete, DM can click the deck to restart the next round. How long will it take your party to escape?

Card Types

  • Right Path

    Finding this card means you are one step closer to escaping the dungeon!

  • Dead End

    You and your party have runinto a dead end, you' will have to find another way to go

  • Monster

    Your party runs the risk or encountering a monster! Can your party make it out alive

    * When a "Monster Card" is found twice, it triggers an encounter

  • Treasure

    Your party finds something awesome!

  • Trap

    Your party has to escape one of the many boobie traps in the dungeon! Will you survive?
