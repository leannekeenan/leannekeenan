Dungeon Crawl

The Card Game
Action Effect
Forge [STR] Add 2 additional Right Path cards to the discard pile from outside the game.
Scout [DEX] Reveal the top card of the deck in addition to a card in the river, choose one
Investigate [INT] Draw 3 cards from the deck, choose 1, and put it on the bottom of the deck. Shuffle the remaining cards and place them on top of the deck.
Consider [WIS] Reveal 2 cards in the river this turn and choose one.
Boost [CHA] Remove a Dead End card in the river from the game. Draw a new card from the deck and place it face down in the river.
