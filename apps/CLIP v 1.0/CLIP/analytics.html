Shift Analytics Dashboard

This comprehensive dashboard is designed to provide valuable insights into your time management and work patterns. Here, you can track and analyze your time spent on breaks, lunches, and at each campus across various timeframes

    Key Features:
  1. Analytics Breakdown View your time allocation for breaks, lunches, and campus locations on a daily, weekly, monthly, and annual basis.
  2. Enhanced Time Management Utilize the insights from the analytics to optimize your work schedule and improve productivity.
  3. Performance Tracking: Monitor your adherence to break and lunch policies, ensuring compliance with company guidelines.
  4. Location Insights: Understand your time spent at different campuses, helping you manage your work locations more effectively.
